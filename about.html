My Background

Hello and welcome to my own personal portfolio website. My name is Lauren Tharpe, a student who is currently studying at the University of Winnipeg. My dream is not only to dive into the computer science field as a whole, but to create meaningful computer science related content for the world to enjoy whether it be a personal passion project or a company-run change in the technology we use today... Currently, I am working towards a bachelors degree in Applied Computer Science. Thus far I have gained experience in Python, Java, HTML, CSS, and Blender. I'd like to say I'm quick to pick up new topics as a lot of my skills, including the ones I used to build this website, are self-taught. I've worked on projects that range in all sorts of required skills, like a discord chatbot taught with an AI, multiple Java and Python based games, as well as a few webscrapers.
